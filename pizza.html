Pizza Recipe

Description

The pizza is one hell of a recipe. First invented in Italy, then perfected by them again. Without pizza this world would be a tough place to live in.

Which is why, we are bringing to you today, the most bad ass pizza recipe in the world, the American Pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Stretch your dough into a circle.
  2. Put the tomato sauce on it.
  3. Put a cup of cheese on the pizza.
  4. Put the SECOND CUP OF CHEESE on the pizza.
  5. Bake in the oven.
  6. Voila!
